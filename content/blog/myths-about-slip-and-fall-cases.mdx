## Misconceptions and Explain

Slip and fall accidents are often misunderstood, with myths deterring victims from seeking legal help. For instance, many people believe that warning signs negate liability or that minor injuries don’t qualify for claims. This post debunks these misconceptions and explains the true criteria for premises liability cases.

If you’ve been injured due to someone else’s negligence, filing a personal injury claim can help you recover compensation for medical bills, lost wages, and pain and suffering. This post explains the process, from documenting the incident and seeking medical attention to negotiating with insurance companies.

We’ll also highlight common pitfalls, such as missing deadlines or accepting lowball settlement offers. Consulting a personal injury attorney ensures that your claim is strong and that you receive the compensation you deserve.

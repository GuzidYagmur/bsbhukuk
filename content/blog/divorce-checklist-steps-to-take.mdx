## Divorce is not just an Emotional

Divorce is not just an emotional decision; it’s a legal process that requires preparation. This post provides a checklist to help individuals get organized, from gathering financial records to understanding property division laws in your state. Proper preparation can save time, money, and unnecessary stress during the proceedings.

## Discuss Emotional Readiness

We’ll also discuss emotional readiness, such as seeking support from friends, family, or counselors, and understanding the impact of divorce on children. By planning ahead, you can navigate the process with clarity and confidence.
